A monthly meetup and gathering of independent game developers by heart in Bangkok.

Come and share your enthusiasm, knowledge, tips/technique, best practices, games, and expand your connection at the same time. Get to know people, help each other and move forward together. Let's make the independent game community in Bangkok strong and better.

Get Involved

ENTHUSIASM

Independent by heart, come here and share your enjoyment in making a living from it with others.

SHARING

Participate as a speaker, or just come and meet up with other fellows. Share what you know, learn from others, then build something great.

Networking

Expand your connection. Connect with other fellows. Possibility is endless either in collaboration or partnership.
